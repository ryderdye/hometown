Top three activities to do in Miami

Go bar hopping

There are tons of great bars in Miami Beach, Wynwood, and Brickell. Las Rosas, The Sylvester, Tea Room, Swizzle, and many more.

Spend the day at the beach

South Beach is beatiful but Key Biscayne and North Miami Beach are great if you're looking for a more calm/quiet beach experience.

The Design District

Spend the day shopping at the luxury fashion and jewelry boutiques, visit the art galleries, and appreciate the unique architecture.
